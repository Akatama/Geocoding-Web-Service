Welcome to SuperGeocoder

This online geocoding service effortlessly transforms all address data within your files into precise latitude and longitude coordinates. Upon uploading your file, you can instantly preview your table on this website or opt to download the file in its converted format. Additionally, our mapping service enriches your experience by enabling you to visualize the exact location of each address, offering deeper insights into each site.

To facilitate the file processing, please ensure that your file includes at least one column that contains the keyword address in its name, regardless of letter casing. View an example of a valid file format here.

We currently only support CSV and XLSX file formats.

To begin, simply click the 'Choose file' button below to select your file, and then hit 'Submit' to start the upload process.
